Data Deletion & Account Safety

App Name: Vichār

Developer Contact: minebusinessdev@gmail.com

At Vichār, we take your data control seriously. In alignment with Google Play’s Data Safety policies, you have the right to request the permanent removal of your account and any personal information associated with it.

Steps to Request Data Deletion

You can choose to delete your data via two separate pathways:

  1. In-App Deletion (Recommended): Open the Vichār app, go to Settings > Account Profile, and click on "Delete Account". Your request will process instantly.
  2. Web/Email Request: If you have already uninstalled the app, send an explicit request from your registered email address to minebusinessdev@gmail.com with the subject line Vichār Account Deletion Request. Please include your username to accelerate verification.

Data Handling, Deletion, and Retention Specifics

Below is the detailed breakdown of what happens to your information when an account deletion request is processed:

Data Type Collected Deletion Status Retention Period & Exceptions
Account Credentials
(Usernames, Passwords)		 Permanently Deleted Purged from our authentication databases immediately upon account deletion.
User Profiles & Identifiers
(Email Addresses)		 Permanently Deleted Wiped completely within 7 business days of an email verification request or instantly via in-app request.
OAuth Tokens
(Third-Party Sign-In Data)		 Revoked & Deleted Authentication states are invalidated immediately. We retain no persistent external keys.
App Diagnostics & Performance Logs Anonymized / Purged Standard server interaction logs are automatically completely cleared or fully anonymized within 90 days.

Additional Policy Notes

We do not retain your personal data after account deletion for any commercial purposes. Data is only held temporarily during the 90-day structural rotation cycles of automated system backups, after which it is fully overwritten.

For further clarity or inquiries regarding your data footprint, contact us directly at minebusinessdev@gmail.com.
