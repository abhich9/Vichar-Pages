How to Request Full Data Deletion

You can trigger a complete cryptographic wipe of your data via two methods:

  1. In-App Deletion (Instant): Open the Vichār app, navigate to Settings > Privacy Dashboard, and select "Delete All User Data". This instantly triggers our background deletion worker to wipe your cloud and local databases.
  2. Web/Email Request: Send an explicit request from the email address associated with your Firebase account to minebusinessdev@gmail.com. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account?

Executing a deletion request triggers a comprehensive wipe across our infrastructure:

Data Category Deletion Process Retention / Exceptions
Cloud Data (Firestore) Permanently Wiped Your user document, synced transcripts, notes, and tier status are instantly deleted from Firebase.
Local Device Data (ObjectBox) Permanently Wiped Local knowledge graphs, AI chat history, tasks, and extracted vocab/topics are destroyed.
Audio Recordings Permanently Wiped All raw audio files stored in the app's internal directory are deleted.
Third-Party Integrations Tokens Revoked Encrypted OAuth tokens (Notion, GitHub, Slack) are purged from your secure keystore.

Automated Retention & Cleanup Schedules

  • Recycle Bin: Items moved to the trash are held for 30 days before our automated TrashCleanupWorker permanently purges them.
  • Short/Blank Recordings: Audio snippets flagged for manual review have a 24-hour advisory Time-To-Live (TTL).
  • Transcript Revisions: AI-processed edit histories of your notes are pruned automatically after 30 days to save space.
