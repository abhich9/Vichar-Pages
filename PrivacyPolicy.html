Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2026

1. Data Storage & Cloud Synchronization

Vichār operates primarily as a local-first application. By default, your audio recordings, transcripts, AI chat logs, and visual knowledge graphs are stored locally on your device in an ObjectBox database. We utilize Firebase (Google) to provide optional cloud synchronization and account authentication.

2. Third-Party AI Processing & Data Sharing

To provide advanced Speech-to-Text (STT) and Large Language Model (LLM) capabilities, Vichār routes your anonymized audio files and text queries through our secure edge-compute proxy (Cloudflare Workers) to third-party AI providers. These include, but are not limited to, Groq, Google Gemini, HuggingFace, and SarvamAI.

  • No AI Training: We explicitly opt out of data training with our API providers where supported. Your recordings and notes are processed for immediate transcription and insight generation, not for training external AI models.
  • Offline Mode Guarantee: Users demanding absolute privacy can enable the "Offline Only" mode in settings. This forces the app to bypass all cloud AI APIs and transcribe audio locally on your device hardware using Whisper.cpp.

3. "What Vichār Learns" (Privacy Dashboard Controls)

Vichār is designed to learn your habits, vocabulary, and topics over time to improve AI chat responses. You maintain granular control over this data extraction. Inside the in-app Privacy Dashboard, you can explicitly disable the AI from extracting or storing:

  • Vocabulary and recurring phrases
  • Topics and personal interests
  • People, places, and relationships (Knowledge Graph nodes)
  • Habit patterns

4. Third-Party Integrations & Security

If you connect third-party productivity tools (e.g., Notion, Google Docs, Slack, GitHub), Vichār stores the corresponding OAuth authentication tokens locally on your device. [Certain] These tokens are secured using AES-256-GCM encryption within Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences and are never transmitted to our servers.

5. Data Deletion

You have the absolute right to purge your data. You can delete your account and execute a full cryptographic wipe of your local and cloud data at any time via the in-app settings. For more details on retention timelines, visit our Data Safety Page.
