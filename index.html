Vichār

Published by minebusinessdev@gmail.com

Vichār (ThoughtFlow) is an advanced, local-first productivity tool that turns your voice into structured knowledge.

  • Intelligent Voice Notes: Capture audio instantly. Vichār uses a multi-engine fallback system (Groq, HuggingFace, Sarvam) to guarantee high-accuracy transcription in English, Hindi, and Hinglish.
  • Visual Knowledge Graph: Watch your ideas connect automatically. Our edge AI extracts topics, tasks, and entities to build a visual mind map of your thoughts.
  • RAG Chat Assistant: Don't just take notes—interrogate them. Chat with your personal AI assistant to query past transcripts and extract cross-referenced insights.
  • Total Privacy Control: Enable "Offline Only" mode to process audio entirely on-device using local Whisper models, bypassing the cloud completely.
