Visual Knowledge Graph

Watch your ideas connect automatically. Our edge AI extracts topics, tasks, and entities to build a visual mind map of your thoughts.

RAG Chat Assistant

Don't just take notes—interrogate them. Chat with your personal AI assistant to query past transcripts and extract cross-referenced insights.

Total Privacy Control

Enable "Offline Only" mode to process audio entirely on-device using local Whisper models, bypassing the cloud completely. Secure AES-256 local encryption is available.
