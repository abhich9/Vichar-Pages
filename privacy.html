Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Vichār mobile application (hereby referred to as "Application") and outlines how we handle user data in strict compliance with Google Play Store policies.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide account authentication and synchronized features, the Application collects specific user data during account creation and login states:

  • Account Identifiers: Usernames, password hashes, and email addresses provided during registration.
  • Third-Party Authentication (OAuth): Profile information shared securely when signing in via third-party providers (e.g., Google Sign-In).

2. Data Security & Transfer

Your trust is paramount. We implement industry-standard protocols to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the Application and our services is secured using cryptographic protocols (HTTPS/TLS), preventing unauthorized interception.

3. Data Deletion & Retention Rights

You maintain full control over your personal data. You have the right to request the complete erasure of your account information at any time.

How to Request Deletion:
Users can permanently delete their accounts directly within the Application's settings panel, or submit an explicit request via email to minebusinessdev@gmail.com. Detailed procedures and retention schedules can be reviewed on our dedicated Data Safety Page.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. You are advised to review this page occasionally for any modifications.
